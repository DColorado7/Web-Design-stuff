What is Soccer?

Soccer is a sport where there are 11 players on the field and they play for 90 minutes trying to kick the ball into the opponents net. The net is protected by an individual called the goalkeeper. Soccer, while not popular in the U.S., is the most popular sport in the world. Every country has a some form of soccer league of their own. You can check out some of the popular ones below.
